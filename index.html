About Us

Welcome to Sip N Spin Bar & Grill

Sip N Spin Bar & Grill brings classic American bar food to downtown Hays with juicy burgers, loaded fries, chicken wings, and ice-cold drinks in a historic atmosphere.

Family-friendly daytime kitchen, lively evenings with darts, pool tables, and live entertainment. Perfect spot for game day or casual hangouts.

Established local favorite serving Hays since years - from steak philly fries to daily specials, everything's made fresh daily.
